# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this project!

## Project Status

This is a personal project, and I may not actively maintain or review pull requests regularly. While I appreciate contributions and suggestions, please understand that:

- Pull requests may take time to review (if at all)
- I may not be actively accepting new features
- This project may be in maintenance mode or primarily for personal use

## How to Contribute

If you'd like to contribute:

1. **Fork the repository** to your own account
2. **Create a branch** for your changes
3. **Make your changes** and test them
4. **Submit a pull request** with a clear description of what you changed

## Expectations

- **No guarantee of merge**: PRs are welcome, but not guaranteed to be merged
- **No timeline**: There's no expected timeline for review
- **Feel free to fork**: If you need changes, feel free to fork and maintain your own version!

## Questions or Issues

If you have questions or find bugs, feel free to open an issue. Just note that I may not respond quickly (or at all) depending on my availability.

## Forking for Your Own Use

This project is MIT licensed, so you're absolutely free to:

- Fork it for your own use
- Modify it as needed
- Use it in your projects
- Build upon it

Thanks for understanding, and feel free to use this code however the MIT license permits!
